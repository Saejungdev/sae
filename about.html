All About Sae Jung


A Field Guide to Understanding Why You Should Choose Sae



Sae Jung was born in the remote country of South Korea. Growing up, he took an interest in computers and in web development from a young age. From the early Windows systems running on floppy disks, Notepad, and Microsoft Word, Sae Jung was fascinated by computers and the inner workings of software in general.

The early internet was a fascinating world. Back in the days of Geocities and Bulletin Board Systems, you could find a lot of interesting websites, from pictures of Alaska to obscure microcontrollers, with the aid of Google, Yahoo, and directory lists. Making and editing websites was easier than ever, and a fun hobby to pass the time before this extremely interconnected age. Before Facebook, Tinder, and Twitch, the most you had to connect you to the outside world was Myspace, which could have its outlook altered with HTML editing. It was a world where you could show off your coding skills by changing icons on your profile.

The Internet, how it works, the webpages contained within, the connections made possible by it, the software that allows it to run, the science behind the servers, and everything else about the online world has always fascinated Sae, and he has strived to learn about it, and computer science always brings him joy.

Sae Jung has been building websites since 2015. Sae started on simple HTML/CSS based sites and gradually evolved to add functionality and database interaction with PHP and SQL. Using an Apache web server and a mySQL database, he has experimented with the back-end inner workings of web development. With jQuery and CSS he has witnessed firsthand how the metaphorical HTML skeleton backbone of a website can be (figuratively) brought to life, adding an interactive skin and musculature of JavaScript to make a website not only worth visiting, but also compelling Sae to revisit again just to gawk at the beauty. If you only knew how fun it is to study such a complex and mind-tickling subject, who knows, maybe you too would decide to make Web Development your profession.

Besides Computer Science and related topics, Sae loves exploration, architecture, and culinary topics. An avid collector of pictures of buildings, scenery, foods, and fridges, Sae satisfies his craving for the world by browsing photographs and collecting memorable pictures.
